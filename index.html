CHARITY FOUNDATION

Charity Foundation is a nonprofit organisation that supports poor children to live healthy life. We bridge the gap between donors and NGOs who are involved in the same cause. Charity Foundation founded during pandemic when the poor families were struggling for a time meal.
We exist purely to pursue our vision and aim, not to grow for the sake of perpetuating our existence. To improve infants’ and young children’s nutrition to ensure their full cognitive and physical development by holistically empowering mothers.
To provide a continuum of services to the most vulnerable peri-urban communities. We want to create sustainable solutions for these communities enabling them to ensure good health, nutrition, and overall economic growth.

VISION

"Our Vision is to ensure the zero under five mortality rate. To make India a country free from any stain of clidren malnutrition. We want that every indian children live healthy & respected life and can also dream of rising higher and For the first time in the history of civilisation, mankind has the capacity to combat and defeat malnutrition at a global scale. we can vastly improve the lives of countless millions of poor people – while also stimulating global economic growth, expanding markets and rewarding enlightened social investors."

AIM

"Aim of Charity Fundation is to make India free from malnutrition and to contibute in making India a world leader full of healthy minds. Charity Foundation wishes to through out the devil of misery and illness from every corner of India and to make highly nutritious “ready-to-use food” products more accessible and affordable to those who need them most, while also increasing awareness of their benefits and therefore generating demand."
we want to fight malnutrition before it occurs. If undernutrition occurs during the first 1000 days, it creates irreversible damages even if it’s treated afterward. So, we empower mothers to act preventively and have a real community-level impact on poverty.
